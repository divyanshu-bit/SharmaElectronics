Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

Is there a visiting charge?
Yes, a nominal visiting charge applies for diagnosis. However, if you proceed with the repair service, this amount is adjusted in the final bill.
Do you provide warranty on repairs?
Absolutely. We provide a 3-6 month warranty on all spare parts replaced and a 30-day service guarantee on our labor.
Which areas do you serve?
We primarily serve North Delhi, including Burari, Pitampura, Rohini, Azadpur, and nearby sectors.
Do you repair old/discontinued models?
Yes, Mr. Sharma specializes in legacy systems. We can often source parts or repair the PCB at the component level for older inverters and stabilizers.
